Privacy Policy

How Inspire Arts Software handles your data across every app, game and platform we make. Last updated 12 July 2026.

The Short Version

Our software collects nothing.

Every Inspire Arts app runs entirely on your own device. None of them create an account, show ads, or contain third-party analytics or tracking, and none send your files to us. The only thing that ever leaves your machine is a StreamHub broadcast — and that goes straight to the streaming service you choose, never through us. The table below spells out exactly what each app does.

Who We Are

The people behind the software.

Inspire Arts Software is the software arm of Inspire Arts, an independent studio. For any privacy question, or to ask us to delete data an app of ours holds, email ukmd001@gmail.com.

Per App

What each app accesses.

Our apps fall into a few groups. This is what each group does with data.

Runs fully on your device — no data collectedWave Studio Plus, AudioViz, Capture, PC PicDraw, PicDraw Grid, PC Pro and the Digital Clock Screensaver all run entirely on your own machine. They open files you choose and save output you ask for. None of them create an account or send anything to Inspire Arts.
Camera & microphone (used live, on your device)AudioViz and Capture use your microphone or system audio; Wave Studio Plus records from your microphone; PicDraw Grid uses the camera to show your reference photo. This audio and video is processed live on your device and only saved where you ask.
Files you create stay with youCapture saves recordings as MP4, Wave Studio Plus saves audio projects, and PicDraw / PicDraw Grid save image copies — always to a location you pick on your own device. Nothing is uploaded.
Streaming to a service you choose (StreamHub)StreamHub sends your live video and audio to the streaming destination you configure (YouTube, Twitch, Facebook or a custom RTMP server). That stream goes directly to your chosen service under its own privacy policy — it never passes through Inspire Arts.
System information (PC Pro)PC Pro reads local hardware and performance data to monitor and optimise your PC. That information stays on your machine and is never transmitted.
Permissions

Why an app might ask for access.

Camera — to display your live reference view (PicDraw Grid). Microphone / audio — for recording and audio features (Wave Studio Plus, AudioViz, Capture, StreamHub). Photos / storage — to open the images you pick and save copies you ask for (PicDraw, PicDraw Grid). You can decline any permission in your device settings; the related feature simply won’t be available.

Ads & Analytics

None. Really.

We do not display advertising, we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not sell or share personal data with anyone.

Your Rights & Data Retention

You stay in control.

Because everything our apps handle stays on your own device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app’s data or removing your files. Where the UK GDPR / EU GDPR applies, you have the right to access, correct or erase personal data and to object to its processing; contact us and we will help with anything within our control.

Changes

If this policy changes.

We may update this policy as our apps evolve. The “last updated” date at the top always reflects the current version, and material changes will be noted here.

Contact

Get in touch.

Questions about this policy or your data? Email ukmd001@gmail.com and we’ll respond.
